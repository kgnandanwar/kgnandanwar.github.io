Kunal Nandanwar

I am a graduate student at Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI) at Robotics Engineering Department. I completed my undergrad from Birla Institute of Technology & Science, Pilani (India), where I worked with Prof. B. K. Rout at The Centre for Robotics & Intelligent Systems(CRIS). I also co-founded Team Garuda, a design team to bring passionate undergraduates together to work on technically challenging projects.

My research interest lies within the areas of Deep Learning & Visual Perception.

Previously, I was associated with Manipulation & Environmental Robotics Lab as a reserch assistant. Currently I am working as a research intern at Honda Research Institute, US.
